How it works

  • How do I create a wallet?
    To start, add Freighter's extension to your browser. Then, create a new wallet and save the 12 words. These words let you access your account on different browsers. Remember, our platform only works on TESTNET. You can fund with friendbot using the extension without involving real money.
  • How do I start a project?
    To create your project, click on "New Project" after connecting your wallet. You can edit your project on its page. If you reach your goal, the admin transfers the donated lumens after the expiration date. If the goal isn't reached, funds go back to backers. You'll see all your projects on "My Account."
  • How can I make a donation?
    You can contribute to any project except your own. The project's page displays all projects, you can search by category or name. If a project misses its goal, you get your donation back. Otherwise, lumens go to the project's creator. Check your donations on "My Account" page.
  • How the admin account works?
    All projects are shown on "Control Panel" page. Select the projects that you want to fund or refund and click on the respective action button. You can't do both simultaniously, it's configured to make only fund or refund transactions. For testing purposes, admin can donate to specific projects.
  • How the transactions work?
    Each transaction has one or more operations. Every donation has a single operation per transaction, but the admin can fund or refund multiple projects, so it can be one or more operations per transaction.
  • How the updates work?
    A lambda function updates project status daily. All the transactions and projects save instantly on RDS and S3 databases.
  • Can I test it on localhost?
    Absolutely! Freighter requires HTTPS for signing transactions. For testing in Flask, a self-signed certificate is fine.
